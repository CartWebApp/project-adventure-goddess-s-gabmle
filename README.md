# The-Goddess-s-Gamble
## Welcome to the Goddess' Gamble! This is a coinflip based RPG game using HTML, CSS, and Javascript. 
In the game, you click to traverse the world, unlock secrets and battle enemies.Your flip determines whether you get to attack or not and flipping again doubles your damage! (yes it stacks). You are given quests by a prophet and depending how you complete them, you gain or lose __*karma*__
