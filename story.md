Intro:
Cutscene starts with the adventurer in a dream and visualizing his childhood in the background. Appearing and disappearing text gives the following lore with each sentence only being shown one at a time. The adventurer does not know who is speaking to him, nor does the player. The player wakes up.

The player checks on mom and then leaves to find her medicine for her illness upon her request.

The player lays low and tries to avoid guards, eventually gets caught in Herbalist’s hut and taken to the castle. Escapes guard.

As you are escaping, cutscene starts showing the breaking of the barrier between dimensions. The main antagonist appears and ravages the city. Timer appears and you must escape the Castle in time. This sequence will be rigged and very rarely will the player actually escape.

You run into a yapping guy and depending on how you react to him is the cutscene that plays & the amount of Karma you will start with.

1-A: Pick Goddess (+Karma). The Goddess is now your internal monologue.
 
1-B: Pick Dragon (-Karma). The Dragon is now your internal monologue.

1-C: Pick Yourself (Dragon gets angry and forces you to repick but doesn’t give you + or - Karma at the start). Whoever you pick the second time will be your internal monologue. You can’t pick yourself again because you won’t be allowed, but it does trigger the 3rd ending (given you did the right actions along the way).

Proceed to 1-1.

1-2: 

The Prophet sends you off to the emperor of Whitoria “Go seek out the Emperor of Whitoria, he is in need of help” Player: “Okay got it.”. The emperor requests you protect the family heirloom because they were tipped off by someone that a robbery would occur and they are low on guards “I was notified by intel that someone would come and steal my family heirloom. I need you to protect it, and don’t worry, you will be paid handsomely”.

1-2A: You protect the Heirloom overnight and get paid (+Karma). The emperor of Whitoria and Whitoria’s people favor you. Whitoria vendors give you a discount on their wares. Go to 1-3.

1-2B: You steal the Heirloom at night and leave the vicinity (-Karma). The emperor hates you and the emperor sends a soldier to fight you and you're supposed to win. Any soldiers you interact with automatically engage in combat with (only in whitoria). You have to now sneak in in order to use the shops in Whitoria and can no longer use the main entrance. In exchange for stealing the Heirloom, you can sell it for a lot of money (or use it as a weapon but it won’t be that good). Go to 1-3.9

1-3: The Prophet sends you to fight Icarus (-Karma) or Astro (+Karma). Bosses affect your karma twice as much as a normal quest “Go check out my bounty board and see who you want to fight. They seem to be causing trouble in the fluctuations between luck and unluck.” Player: “Uhh okay”.

1-3A: You fight Astro (++Karma). “You fool, the dragon will have your head…” You gain the dragon’s wings.
1-3B: You fight Icarus (--Karma). “Damn you. Don’t you realize what you’re doing?” You gain Icarus’ wings.

If you choose the Goddess, she tells you to go to the mountain and fight the Dragon. If you choose the Dragon, he tells you to go to the mountain and fight the Goddess. However, if you chose yourself as the first choice, you are told by an unknown person (named ??? in game) that tells you you must fight both the dragon and goddess ???: “Go defeat them both. They have done nothing for you in your quest to greatness.” “Wh-Who is this??” ???: “No need to worry who I am. Just on who YOU are.”  . The player/adventurer does not know who spoke to them but they follow their directions anyway. From this point forward, you no longer gain or lose Karma. The amount you have now will be solidified and your ending is set in stone.

1-9-2: You can find a mystical anvil somewhere in the wilderness that can be used to craft exclusive weapons. If you did ONLY GOOD choices, you can use those items to craft the Good-Path-Exclusive Weapon. Vice Versa for the ONLY BAD choices. However, if you did a mixture of both good and bad choices, you can go to a shaman in a secret spot that converts your good and bad items into an exclusive NEUTRAL weapon. YOU MUST CRAFT THIS ITEM TO GET THE THIRD ENDING.
